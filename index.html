--- layout: default title: "Tattvavada: A primer on Dvaita" ---

Tattvavada is a school of Vedantic thought that promulgates theism, realism and plurality. It was founded by Madhvāchāryā as a direct reaction against the tenets of Advaitā. The site is a repository of articles on the philosophy of Madhvā and his followers but not restricted to it. The articles will focus on the broader implications of the philosophy on the people through history and culture. The articles are linked below. In the appendices, I will expand upon the tools I will be using to interpret the philosophy and its impact. Please read the preface before proceeding. As the articles require deep research, I will not work on a time-honoured basis. They will be uploaded when they organically reach a stage of completion. I hope the site is of some use to you. Thank you for your time!
